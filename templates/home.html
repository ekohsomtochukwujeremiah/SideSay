MessGo is a real-time messaging sanctuary built on the philosophy of ephemeral communication. In a world where everything is recorded, we offer the freedom of transience. Connect securely using unique keys or usernames, manage your private profile, and experience privacy by design. Your words belong to the moment, not the server.
